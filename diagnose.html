Vestibular Maturity Model Framework

This model connects a team maturity diagnostic lens with modular progression.

Stage 1: Foundational

  • Diagnostic lens: Chaotic → teams drowning in tool sprawl, unclear workflows, burnout signals ignored.
  • Focus: Restore clarity, reduce cognitive load, establish hygiene.
  • Outcomes: Reduced clutter, clear workflows, initial alignment.
  • Deliverables: Tool hygiene audit, sanitation playbook.
  • Package: Declutter.
  • AI augmentation: Automated Jira board cleanup, duplicate detection, backlog hygiene assistant.

Stage 2: Emerging

  • Diagnostic lens: Structured → boards cleaned, cadence exists, but ceremonies feel stale.
  • Focus: Team health, engagement, morale-first practices.
  • Outcomes: Improved trust, reduced burnout, meaningful rituals.
  • Deliverables: Morale metrics dashboard, role clarifications, team health interventions.
  • Package: Reboot.
  • AI augmentation: Retro documentation, sprint flow insights, facilitation prompts.

Stage 3: Enabled

  • Diagnostic lens: Modular → frameworks adopted, backlog hygiene maintained, morale engagement rising.
  • Focus: Pilot modular Agile practices with one team, refine cadence, prepare for scale.
  • Outcomes: Autonomous teams, faster iteration, clear accountability.
  • Deliverables: Team-specific enablement plan, coaching cadence.
  • Package: Enable.
  • AI augmentation: Backlog prioritization rubric, automated work item creation, sprint health dashboards.

Stage 4: Scaling

  • Diagnostic lens: Sustainable (early) → teams self-manage hygiene, cross-team visibility emerging.
  • Focus: Replicate modular practices across teams, align governance.
  • Outcomes: Scalable Agile practices, consistent delivery, transparency.
  • Deliverables: Scaled rollout plan, cross-team coaching, governance map.
  • Package: Scale.
  • AI augmentation: Cross-team dashboards, compliance reporting, predictive bottleneck detection.

Stage 5: Optimizing

  • Diagnostic lens: Sustainable (advanced) → predictive dashboards, audit-ready compliance, continuous improvement culture.
  • Focus: Strategic agility, continuous improvement, business alignment.
  • Outcomes: Sustained momentum, strategic alignment, high morale.
  • Deliverables: Advanced dashboards, strategic workshops, compliance frameworks.
  • Package: Optimize.
  • AI augmentation: Predictive analytics, strategic scenario modeling, continuous improvement dashboards.
